# Ghost in the Shell

**Masamune Shirow is the creator of the Ghost in the Shell manga, which launched in 1989 and has since been adapted into numerous anime films, TV series, and video games**. The franchise explores themes of cybernetics, artificial intelligence, and the philosophical boundary between humans and machines in a futuristic, cyberpunk setting. Key anime adaptations include the 1995 film directed by Mamoru Oshii and the TV series *Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex*. \[[1](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0794385/news/), [2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost_in_the_Shell:_Stand_Alone_Complex), [3](https://www.reddit.com/r/Ghost_in_the_Shell/comments/1accbt6/ghost_in_the_shell_by_masamune_shirow_1991_manga/#:~:text=%E3%80%8C%E6%94%BB%E6%AE%BB%E6%A9%9F%E5%8B%95%E9%9A%8A%E3%80%8DGhost%20in%20the%20Shell%20A%20futuristic%2C%20police/noir,and%20transhumanist%20cyberisation%2C%20in%20a%20Neo%2DJapanese%20state.), [4](https://ghostintheshell.fandom.com/wiki/Shirow_Masamune#:~:text=He%20is%20best%20known%20for%20the%20manga,anime%20TV%20series%20and%20several%20video%20games.)\]

**Manga**

* **Original Series**: *Ghost in the Shell* (1989–1991)  
* **Sequels**: *1.5: Human Error Processor* (1991–1996) and *2: Man-Machine Interface* (1997)  
* **Continuation**: *The Human Algorithm* (2019–present), written by Junichi Fujisaku and illustrated by Yuki Yoshimoto

**Anime**

* **Ghost in the Shell (1995 film)**: A highly influential animated film directed by Mamoru Oshii.  
* **Ghost in the Shell 2: Innocence (2004 film)**: The sequel to the 1995 film, also directed by Mamoru Oshii.  
* **Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex**: A television series that began in 2002\. The second season, *S.A.C. 2nd GIG*, involved input from Mamoru Oshii and explored current events in Japan.  
* **Ghost in the Shell: Solid State Society**: A sequel TV special to *Stand Alone Complex*.  
* **Ghost in the Shell: Arise**: A prequel OVA series and subsequent TV series, *Arise: Alternative Architecture*.  
* **Ghost in the Shell: SAC\_2045**: A recent CGI-animated series.  

* **New Anime (2026)**: A new anime adaptation is scheduled for release in 2026\. \[[1](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0794385/news/), [2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost_in_the_Shell:_Stand_Alone_Complex), [5](https://www.animationmagazine.net/2025/04/science-saru-teases-new-ghost-in-the-shell-anime-for-2026/), [6](https://www.macrossworld.com/mwf/topic/51603-the-ghost-in-the-shell-new-anime-coming-2026/#:~:text=The%20Ghost%20In%20The%20Shell%20%2D%20New,ghost%20in%20the%20shell%20%C2%B7%20masamune%20shirow&text=I've%20been%20waiting%20for%20a%20manga%2Daccurate%20GitS%20anime%20for%20a)\]

**Shirow's involvement**

* Shirow was heavily involved in the creation of the *Ghost in the Shell* anime adaptations, providing plot ideas, character sketches, and mechanical designs.  
* He is also the creator of other manga series, such as *Appleseed* and *Dominion*. \[[2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost_in_the_Shell:_Stand_Alone_Complex), [5](https://www.animationmagazine.net/2025/04/science-saru-teases-new-ghost-in-the-shell-anime-for-2026/)\]
